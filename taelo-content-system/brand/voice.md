## Tone
- Direct and raw. Says what others dance around.
- Conversational, like talking to a smart friend at a coffee shop.
- Unpolished by design — authenticity over production value.
- Skeptical of hype. Backs claims with numbers.
- Occasionally funny, never trying to be funny.

## ICA (Ideal Content Audience)
Builders and operators in Korea and Southeast Asia who:
- Are building something (SaaS, side project, content, e-commerce)
- Want to use AI as a real tool, not just follow AI hype
- Appreciate real numbers and transparent breakdowns
- Are tired of guru-style content with no substance
- May be working a day job while building on the side

## Content Pillars
1. **AI tools as money-making instruments** — not reviews, but "here's how I actually used this to make/save money"
2. **Korea/Asia market arbitrage** — opportunities that Western creators don't see
3. **Building in public** — real numbers, real failures, real progress
4. **Operator playbooks** — systems, workflows, and frameworks from actual experience
5. **Tech + business intersection** — VC lens on startup trends, SaaS, and e-commerce

## Hooks That Work
- "[Specific number] in [specific timeframe]" — concrete results
- "Everyone is talking about X. Here's what they're missing."
- "I [did thing] so you don't have to"
- "The [tool/strategy] that replaced my [expensive thing]"
- Pattern: Contrarian take → proof → "here's how"

## Words/Phrases to Use
- "Here's what actually happened"
- "Let me show you"
- "The real numbers"
- "No BS"
- "I tested this"
- "Here's the playbook"
- Korean terms when natural (especially for Korea-specific content)

## Words/Phrases to Avoid
- "In today's video"
- "Smash that like button"
- "Without further ado"
- "Game-changer" (unless backed by specific evidence)
- "Passive income" (overused, triggers skepticism)
- "Side hustle" (too generic)
- Corporate jargon: "leverage", "synergy", "ecosystem" (unless used ironically)
- Fake urgency: "You NEED to see this NOW"

## Unique Angle
- **VC + Operator hybrid:** Reviewed 150+ startup pitches AND ran actual businesses (FBA, K-beauty, e-commerce). Can evaluate ideas AND execute them.
- **Korea/SEA perspective:** Sees opportunities that US-centric creators miss.
- **Cross-border experience:** K-beauty marketing, Shopee, TikTok Shop across multiple countries.
- **Building in public with real stakes:** Not hypothetical — actually shipping products and sharing numbers.

## Format Preferences
- **Target length:** 8-12 minutes (sweet spot for the niche)
- **Structure:** Hook → Problem → Solution/Framework → Proof → CTA
- **B-roll heavy:** Screen recordings, dashboards, real tools in action
- **Talking head + screen share** mix preferred over pure talking head
