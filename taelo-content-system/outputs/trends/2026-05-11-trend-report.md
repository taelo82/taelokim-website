## 📈 Updated growth strategy (beyond IG/X)

Research-backed plays for 2026 specifically. Each is sourced.

### High-leverage (rank order)

#### 1. Shorts-as-funnel with explicit long-form hand-off
Why it works in 2026: YouTube's recommender treats Shorts and long-form as independent surfaces but rewards channels where Shorts viewers cross over. Watch-Through-Rate on the first loop (~70% threshold) is the dominant Shorts metric — not CTR. ([Vugola](https://www.vugolaai.com/blog/how-to-grow-youtube-channel), [LearnWithParth](https://www.learnwithparth.com/blog/youtube-shorts-algorithm-update-2026))
- **First action:** Pull 4 Shorts from each long-form via OpusClip. **Manually rewrite the first 2 seconds** — auto-hooks underperform. Pin a comment with the long-form link. Custom "full build on the channel" end-card.
- **Effort:** Med · **Time to signal:** 1 month

#### 2. Playlist re-architecture for session time
One reported case study: reorganising existing videos into 5 focused playlists pushed a channel from ~4K to 6K watch hours in 2 months with zero new uploads. ([Miraflow](https://miraflow.ai/blog/youtube-playlists-strategy-2026-binge-sessions))
- **First action (one afternoon):** Build 3 themed playlists ("Claude Code from scratch", "AI tool teardowns", "Builds in public"). Set the first video's end-screen to auto-queue the next playlist video. Add playlist links to every description.
- **Effort:** Low · **Time to signal:** 1 month

#### 3. YouTube Communities (Posts) tab with hyperlinks ⭐ underused
Communities rolled out broadly in 2025; posts with hyperlinks are now indexable and surface in the home feed for subs. Most <50K AI creators ignore it. ([YouTube Blog](https://blog.youtube/news-and-events/a-new-youtube-communities-experience-for-fans-by-fans/), [PPC Land](https://ppc.land/youtube-expands-communities-feature-to-all-eligible-creators/))
- **First action:** Day before each drop, post a "what I'm shipping in tomorrow's video — vote on the second tool I should benchmark" poll, with a hyperlink to a previous related video.
- **Effort:** Low · **Time to signal:** 1 week

#### 4. Newsletter cross-pollination (Ben's Bites Pro / TLDR AI / Latent Space / AI Tidbits)
AI newsletter audiences are overwhelmingly builders — exactly Taelo's ICP. Conversion from newsletter → sub is dramatically higher than from cold IG. ([Ben's Bites Pro](https://pro.bensbites.co/), [DataNorth](https://datanorth.ai/blog/top-10-ai-newsletters-to-follow-in-2026))
- **First action:** Cold email 5 newsletter editors with one specific data point from his most recent video (e.g., "I ran Claude Code on X for 14 days, here's the failure-mode chart"). Offer the chart as exclusive embed; ask for a video link in return.
- **Effort:** Med · **Time to signal:** 1 month

#### 5. Reddit + Hacker News "build artifact" posts (NOT video posts) ⭐ highest single-shot upside
r/ClaudeAI, r/LocalLLaMA, r/AI_Agents, and Show HN reject naked video links but reward open-source artifacts, benchmarks, write-ups. The video becomes the "deeper dive" link inside a high-utility post. **A single front-page Show HN can deliver 1–3K subs.** ([KarmaGuy](https://karmaguy.io/en/blog/reddit-self-promotion-rules))
- **First action:** Ship `taeloautomates/claude-video` (the Brad-watch-skill fork) as a Show HN with a 2-line "video walkthrough here" link. Mirror to r/ClaudeAI as "I forked Brad's /watch skill — here's what broke and how I fixed it."
- **Effort:** Med · **Time to signal:** 1 week

### Medium-leverage (briefer)

- **Podcast guesting** — one well-aligned podcast/month outperforms a tour. Targets: Latent Space, Cognitive Revolution, AI Engineer, Super Data Science.
- **"I tested X for 14 days" operator format** — 8–15 min sweet spot for long-form. Operator/case-study videos out-convert tutorials for subs-per-view in AI niche.
- **Discord presence** in Latent Space, AI Engineer, Anthropic Builders — be the helpful answer-er; username is the link.
- **LinkedIn long-form text + native video** — Dwell Time is now LinkedIn's #1 ranking signal in 2026. Repost transcripts as carousels.
- **Pinned comment with timestamps + "next video" question** — measurable retention bump.
- **Native YT thumbnail A/B testing** (now in YT Studio) — face-forward + 3-word text consistently wins in AI-coding niche.
- **Bluesky over Threads for AI niche** — Threads has scale but no AI-builder cluster.

### Don't bother

- **Generic "Claude vs Cursor vs Codex"** — saturated; CTR collapsed late 2025 (and confirmed by Trend 5 in this scan).
- **Threads cross-posting** — no AI-builder cluster.
- **Cron-scheduled scrapers / "compounding intelligence library"** — Taelo's own preference, also fragile.
- **Generic AI-thumbnail generators** — fail at <150px mobile size.
- **Vague "post consistently on TikTok"** — TikTok's AI/coding niche is shallow vs YT and Reddit.

### What changed since the 2024 playbook

1. **Shorts metric shift** — WTR-on-first-loop and ~70% retention threshold replaced raw view count as the Shorts ranking input.
2. **Communities (now "Posts") opened up with clickable hyperlinks** — a real distribution surface, not just sub-only chatter.
3. **Frontier-lab leaders moved primary public comms to long-form podcasts** — guesting on those orbits compounds.
4. **First 30 seconds is now an explicit ranking input** (not just internal benchmark) — reshoot cold-opens, kill the channel-intro animation.
5. **AI auto-clipper quality plateaued** — Opus/Vizard reframe still weak on 2-person podcasts. Manual hook-rewrite is back as the differentiator.
