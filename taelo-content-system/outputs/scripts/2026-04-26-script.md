## Format Note (READ FIRST)
This is **NOT a fixed-length talking-head script**. Taelo records the actual taelokim.com build with OBS, then voices over the talking blocks in this script. Each block has:
- 🎙️ **VO** — voiceover words (read into camera or over screen capture)
- 🖥️ **SCREEN** — what should be visible during the VO
- ⏱️ **Density** — rough VO seconds (so total length is predictable from how many blocks make the cut)

Final video length = sum of VO blocks + however much raw build footage Taelo wants to keep between them. Aim for ~70% talking, 30% silent build footage time-lapsed.

## Recording Notes (for Taelo)

1. **Order of recording:** Record Override 1's build first (cinematic asset gen) since it produces the hero you'll cut back to repeatedly. Then sequentially.
2. **Time-lapse:** Anywhere Claude Design takes 30+ seconds to render, time-lapse to 3-5 seconds. The VO carries continuity.
3. **Cursor visibility:** Show your prompts going in — viewers need to see the actual text. Pause mouse on each block before sending.
4. **Avoid:** Don't apologize on camera ("uh, this might not work"). Edit out stumbles. Default to confident even when iterating.
5. **Korean phrases:** Drop in 1-2 naturally during voice override section if it helps the point land. Don't force.
6. **Length sanity check:** Sum of all VO ⏱️ above ≈ **8 minutes 40 seconds of pure narration**. Add screen-build moments at ~30-50% of that = final video lands somewhere in the **11-13 minute** range. Sweet spot for the niche per `brand/voice.md`.
